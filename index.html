Stage 0: The Phone Call (Appointment Setting)

Objection:

"What is your absolute best price/monthly payment right now?"

Response Options:
**Option A (Value & Inspection):** I'd love to give you a definitive price, but our best price is always based on three factors: the final inspection of your trade, your finance options, and the specific incentives available today. I can lock in the best price for you in just **20 minutes**. Are mornings or afternoons better for a quick stop?
**Option B (Risk Reversal):** I completely understand you're shopping price. To make sure you're comparing apples-to-apples, come in for 15 minutes. If we don't get you a better deal than anyone else, I will personally fill your gas tank for the rest of your shopping trip. When can you stop by today?

Objection:

"I'm too busy/Just send me the info via email."

Response Options:
**Option A (Urgency & Scarcity):** I can certainly send you the information, but the reason I called is because this specific model is extremely popular. I wouldn't want to email you a vehicle that's sold before you even get here. I have a 15-minute slot at 4:00 PM today or 10:00 AM tomorrow. Which one works best?
**Option B (Confirm Commitment):** I respect your time! I'll send the email right now. Just so I know it's worth following up, if everything in that email is exactly what you are looking for, when is the very next time you'd be able to swing by to see it?

Objection:

"I need to check with my spouse/I'll call you back."

Response Options:
**Option A (Schedule the Follow-Up):** That's perfect. Since you're ready to talk to them, let's schedule a time right now for when you're finished talking. I have slots available at 6 PM tonight or 10 AM tomorrow to review everything. Which works better for you to confirm the appointment?
**Option B (Assume the Sale):** I understand. In case you can't reach them, let's secure a guaranteed time slot right now just in case. If you need to change it, no problem, but at least you have the spot locked in to see the car. Shall I pencil you in for tomorrow afternoon?

Objection:

"I'm only calling to confirm if the car is still available."

Response Options:
**Option A (Urgency & Control):** That's a great question! Yes, it's here right now, but we have multiple appointments scheduled to see it today. The only way to guarantee it's here for you is to set a firm appointment. I have a 10:00 AM slot or a 3:30 PM slot. Which is safer for you?
**Option B (Assume the Sale):** I can confirm it's here right now. I'll pull it up front and hold the keys for you. What time are you planning to be here so I can make sure we are ready for the test drive?

Objection:

"My credit is really bad/I already know I won't qualify."

Response Options:
**Option A (Leverage & Confidence):** I completely understand that concern. Here at the dealership, we work with over **30 different lenders**, not just the big banks, and we send them **millions of dollars of business** every year. Because of this volume, we have personal relationships and can often **call in favors** to do more for you than you could ever do on your own. The only way I can put our team to work for you is if you come in for 15 minutes. Can you be here by lunch or after work today?
**Option B (Focus on the Trade):** I hear you. Even if we're just working on your trade value for now, knowing that number is the first step. Can you come in so we can give you a firm, guaranteed trade value? We can talk credit later, but let's lock in your trade price today.

  • **Down Payment:** Concerned about not having the money today? We can often have you sign a **Promissory Note**, giving you **X days to gather the money** and bring it back to us. You can shop today as if you have all the down payment ready!
Knowing that, what is the next step that makes the most sense for you?
